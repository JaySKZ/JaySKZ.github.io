About Me.

My name is Jason Zhou, I'm 5th year Engineering Physics student at UBC, and based in beautiful Vancouver, Canada.

I am passionate about controls, robotics, and how modern advancements in articial intelligence and machine learning can help solve novel problems in autonomous robotics in societal settings. Particularly, I am very interested in aerial robotics/drones and swarm control, and how we can integrate these technologies in modern logistical and transportation systems.

Currently, I am an undergraduate reasearcher under the supervision of Dr. Maryam Kamgarpour in the Electrical and Computer Engineering department at UBC. My research is focused on the exploration and implementation of safe reinforcement learning algorithms for mobile robotics.
