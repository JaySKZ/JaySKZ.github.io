Small Steps


Cross Platform Mental Health Smart Phone Application, Tool for PTSD Therapists

For the Health Hackathon LumoHacks in 2018, my teammates and I created a React Native based app, which functioned as a therapy tracker. Allowing patients to monitor their heart rate and in case of irregularities be notified and log their experience together with location and current HR spike. Then, all the logs will be analyzed and uploaded to a server which if logged as a Therapist, output of each patient including their log, trend graphs and other information will be displayed.
